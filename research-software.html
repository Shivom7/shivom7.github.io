Probabilistic modelling · adapted implementation

Gaussian Processes Joint Modeling

GPflow TensorFlow Latent dynamics Uncertainty

An updated simulation workflow for joint modelling of neural and behavioural observations. The notebook generates synthetic observations, fits a two-dimensional latent process, produces neural and behavioural predictions with uncertainty intervals, and compares recovered spatial, temporal and spatiotemporal kernels with ground truth.

The contribution is practical research-engineering work: reviewing and troubleshooting a legacy pipeline, packaging the required modules together, and reorganizing the simulation so the model-recovery stages are inspectable.

P300, ERP and neural-microstate analysis

A large, configurable workflow for the OpenNeuro ds002893 audiovisual attention dataset. It connects raw-data discovery and preprocessing with P300/ERP measurements, microstate modelling, subject- and group-level statistics, figures and run documentation.

The accompanying MNE notebook documents the preprocessing sequence in a smaller, reviewable form: EDF and event loading, channel metadata, bad-channel handling, filtering, ICA-assisted artefact removal, average referencing, spectral quality control and cleaned-data export.

Human study design · research in development

Mixed audiovisual oddball experiment

PsychoPy English / Hindi Event markers Working memory Reaction time

I recently designed this experimental protocol for a planned cross-sectional study investigating how semantic context may influence candidate markers of ageing across people engaged in different meditation practices.

The protocol combines a five-minute baseline, audiovisual oddball blocks, working-memory and reaction-time tasks, randomized ordering, bilingual instructions, post-task surveys and DASS-21. Timestamped events and explicit section markers are designed to support synchronization with physiological recordings.
